## The Stochastic Reality:  Learning to Dance with Chaos

Traditional engineering is about **eliminating uncertainty. ** AI-augmented development is about **managing it.**

### Building Reliable Systems with Unreliable Components

**Key mindset shifts:**

1. **Embrace iteration over perfection**
   - AI won't get it right the first time
   - Build feedback loops into your workflow

2. **Develop AI debugging instincts**
   - Learn to spot hallucinations quickly
   - Know when to re-prompt vs. manually fix

3. **Test differently**
   - Test the *behavior* of AI-generated code, not just correctness
   - Use AI to generate test cases

4. **Version control your prompts**
   - Treat prompts like code
   - Document what works for future projects

5. **Build guardrails**
   - Use type systems, linters, and tests to catch AI mistakes
   - Human review for critical sections
